# World gen M3c-M3e: real climate stages

**Date:** 2026-06-11

## Summary

The three climate milestones replacing the stub stages that froze every
planet, shipped as three sequential PRs (#130 atmosphere, #131
precipitation/rivers, M3e biomes/snow/summary). Generated planets now
average ~14C on Earth-like params with rainforest-to-polar latitudinal
biome structure, and landing sites read different biomes depending on
where you click (previously: ArcticTundra everywhere, mean -25C).

## Root cause of the frozen planets (M3c)

The stub AtmosphereStage anchored temperature on
`derived.equilibriumTemperatureK` — the pre-greenhouse Stefan-Boltzmann
value (-18C for Earth) — then subtracted a latitude term. The missing
greenhouse contribution (+33C on Earth) is now modeled as
`33C * sqrt(atmosphereStrength)`, with latitude variation distributed as
`A * (cos^2(lat) - 2/3)` around the preserved global mean (the
area-weighted sphere mean of cos^2 is exactly 2/3). Lapse on land only;
seasonal range from latitude/eccentricity/atmosphere/maritime
moderation; wind keeps the three-cell structure with rotation-shifted
band edges and physically scaled speeds.

## M3d: precipitation + rivers

Latitude bands and seeded noise kept from the stub; added
temperature-scaled evaporation, an upwind orographic march along the
wind field (windward boost to 1.6x, rain shadows to 0.25x, interior
drying), and ocean moisture sourcing. River fields moved here from
SnowStage's stub claim: slab-parallel steepest-descent `downhill`,
deterministic elevation-sorted `flowAccum` accumulation (TileId
tie-breaks; bit-identical at any thread count).

## M3e: biomes, snow, summary

Whittaker temperature x precipitation classification covering all 21
biomes with elevation zonation (MontaneForest / AlpineGrassland /
AlpineTundra), wetlands gated on drainage (shared kRiverFlowThreshold),
Beach on low coastal land, kFlagCoast on ocean-adjacent land. Snow
threshold shifts with atmosphere thickness. WorldSummary gets a real
riverTileCount and a weighted habitability heuristic (forests/
grasslands/wetlands/beach 1.0, montane/alpine-grass/xeric 0.5, deserts/
tundra/polar 0). OceanStage stub was confirmed correct, unchanged.

## Verification

  snow added across the milestones), including full-pipeline summary
  checks and 1-vs-5-thread determinism.
- Live: temperature mode shows the equator-pole gradient (mean 13.6C,
  habitability 65% on the verification world); precipitation mode shows
  the ITCZ wet band and subtropical dry belt; biome mode shows
  rainforest/savanna/desert/temperate/tundra banding; landing site
  readouts returned Beach at 43N and TemperateGrassland at 28N on the
  same planet.
